ABOUT US

Our History

As a legal corporation registered under the Companies and Allied Matters Act, 2020 of the Federal Republic of Nigeria, Amaco Global Education Limited (RC 1918719) was founded in 2018 having its head office in Nigeria’s Federal Capital Territory, Abuja. We are dedicated to aiding future Nigerian students to fulfil their academic and career goals, bridging the gap between ideas and reality, and facilitating inbound and outbound travels.

Amaco Global Education became fully registered and operational in 2022 and was founded to fulfil the growing demand among students and professionals for higher standard of education and professional practices, which is heavily reliant on getting placement and positions in cooperative organizations, institutions, and universities abroad.

At the core of our service, is our offer of free counselling to anyone who wants to study and travel abroad but doesn’t know how to go about it. Every good research effort that is suitable for application is welcomed and used by us. We provide specialized student services in areas such as admissions processing, student visa support/assistance, and pre-departure briefing.

THE DRIVE

Our Mission
